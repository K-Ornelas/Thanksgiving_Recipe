Instructions

for Thanksgiving Green Bean Casserole

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Whisk together the milk and cream of mushroom in a mixing bowl.
    • Pro-tip: Pour the cream first, then use your milk to rinse out your can to get all the cream.
  3. Drain and rinse the canned green beans and fold into your cream mixture.
  4. Fold in half of the cheddar cheese.
  5. Fold in 2 ounces of the fried onions.
  6. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish.
  7. Top with the rest of the cheese.
  8. Top with the rest of the fried onions.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes.
